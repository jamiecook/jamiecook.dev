Web Mapping

I really like maps, especially online maps that you can explore. I think that there is something really special about being able to move around and explore data in this way, and that it really enhances my ability to communicate the insights that can be derived from spatial data.

I've been using Mapbox GL JS since about 2017 and playing around with react, deck.gl, kepler.gl on and off over that period as well. Most of the projects I work on are for work, but here is where I've collected together some of my personal side projects.
